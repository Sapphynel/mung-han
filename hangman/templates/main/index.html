Welcome to Hangman

  1. What is Hangman?

    2. Hangman is a word-guessing game.

  2. What are the rules of Hangman?

    3. Glad you asked! The rules are simple: you guess a letter, and hope it's in the word. If it is, great! Guess again. If it isn't? The number of errors racks up by one. Reach ten errors, and you hang.

  3. Is there a reason for being a user?

    4. Currently, this application doesn't count wins and losses for non-users. Becoming a user will allow you to keep track of match history, which also keeps track of your wins and losses.
